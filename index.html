Biography

I am currently a researcher at Robotics Institute, advised by Professor Kris Kitani.

I will be graduating this year with a BS degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering.


My main interest is in deep learning and its application in computer vision.

Currently, I am working on tasks related to video understanding such as action recognition, action detection,

person re-identification, anomaly detection, online action detection, pedestrian tracking, pose estimation and etc.

I am also interested in topics related to model compression, image and video hashing, link prediction in complex network,

robust model against adversarial attack and many more.
