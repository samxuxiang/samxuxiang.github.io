Biography

I am currently a short-term research intern at Baidu Institute of Deep Learning, working with Professor Guodong Guo .

Previously, I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a BS degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

At CMU, I am fortunate to work with Professor Kris Kitani .


My main interest is computer vision and deep learning.

Currently, I am working on video understanding such as action recognition, action detection,

person re-identification, anomaly detection, online action detection, pedestrian tracking, pose estimation and etc.

I am also interested in topics related to model compression, image and video hashing, link prediction in complex network,

robust model against adversarial attack and many more.
